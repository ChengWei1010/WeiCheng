About Wei Cheng

(You can pronounce “Wei” like “way”)

I am a passionate UX designer based in Austin, pursuing MS degree of information science at UT Austin. I am originally from Taiwan and I have 3+ years of frontend design and develop experience. I see myself as a designer who has a developer’s analytical, logical mind.

I am familiar with app and web frontend development, UI and interaction design and prototyping. Currently I’m enhancing UX research knowledge, as well as more design skills.

I am optimistic and energetic, I like to accept challenges and share my experiences with people. I hope that I can design meaningful solutions for users and make them happy.
