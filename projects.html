    • FamiFilm - Android Mobile Application

    Duration

    9 months

    Position

  • Group Leader of 6 people
  • User Interface Designer

    • Tools

  • Sketch, Photoshop
  • Java, Firebase Database

    • Description

    FamiFilm is the graduation capstone project for Information Management Department, National Sun Yat-sen University.
    It is an android mobile application, by using images to represent functions include Group Calendar, Image Phonebook, Playing Video.
    FamiFilm was designed for family members in all ages.

  • Group Calendar provides a shared place for families to add events, users can click on the bell icon to turn on notification.
  • Image Phonebook show each members' photos, clicking on the photos can call that person or edit the contact information.
  • Playing Video shows videos from other family members in that day. By sliding left or right, and clicking on the play button can easily control it.
