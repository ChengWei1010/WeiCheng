DESIGN DIARY

UX Design

Overview

Design Diary is a Medium publication includes 10 design diaries. Each post is an observational diary of user interactions with any information environment. I define a design problem, evaluate, and redesign it. The topic includes mobile application, website, or things in everyday life. The goal is practice user-centered design thinking and practice design tool skills.


Tools

Sketch / Adobe Photoshop / paper sketching


Methods

User Center Design / UX Evaluation / Literature Review


Time

3 Months

Read Design Diary on Medium
Why Design Diary?
Design is not just what it looks like and feels like.
Design is how it works. -- Steve Jobs

Design skill is an important skill for a UX/UI designer like me. And I think there are several skills in the area of design and need to be practice. Writing design diaries require observing things and designs in daily life, which made me more aware of each design elements in things. In addition, after detecting and defining problems, I have chances to give suggestions, redesign in each post using creative and professional ways. I can also practice using different methods to solve problems.

I choose Medium as the platform to post my design diaries because it provides me a place to expand my network and have potential opportunities to discuss with other designers. Feedbacks are important elements in design thinking process as well. Reading other’s feedbacks helps me to improve and as motivations to create more high-quality design diaries.

PROCESS
Observe - Define - Design

Each entry covers the context of occurrence, the nature of the problem from the user’s perspective, an analytical/theoretical framing of the experience, and a clear recommendation for re-design that would improve the experience.

Mostly, I observed designs of websites, mobile applications, and things that I often use by myself in daily life. I also asked other people’s opinions and challenges of using things in their experience and observation. Sometimes I just pick a environment and observed the using behavior of other people.
WHAT'S IN DESIGN DIARY?
The Diary Topics
3 Websites
I selected problems that I faced in using websites. I wrote about design methods and try to use evaluation method to evaluate and improve, including
  • design indicators
  • design consistency
  • usability evaluation.
3 Mobile Apps
Mobile applications are very common. However, there are still some apps don’t start from user-centered design. I discussed mobile applications
  • started from user’s goal
  • mapping and feedback
  • visibility of system status
4 Everyday Things
I explored different factors related to design in everyday things, including
  • the signifier in design
  • UX and ergonomics
  • colors in design
Reflection

I enjoy doing design diary a lot. I found out it easy to criticize on one design, but it is difficult to reason it through a user-center lense and describe the problem in an analytical way. I would made update versions of each design diary and I will keep noticing and observing good and bad designs in life, then in the future I would add more posts.
