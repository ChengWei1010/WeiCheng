NSYSU HERO

game UI design

Background

This Unity 2-Dimentional game was made in a 2 days hackathon competition in Department of Information Management at National Sun Yat-sun University.

NSYSU HERO is based on the geographical background of National Sun Yat-sun University. There are a lot of monkeys on the mountainous campus, and since sometimes the monkeys can be pretty aggressive, campus safeguards would use airgun to scareoff the monkeys preventing them from attacking students. Thus, this game use easy keyboard control the character to interact.

Role

I am the graphic designer in a team of 3 people. I designed elements including the objects, and background in the game scenes.
