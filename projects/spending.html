SPENDING REPORT

Data Visulization

Overview

This is a data visualization design project of my real expenses in 4 years of my college life. From the collection of data, using Tableau to visulize, and turning all the material into a dashboard.

Role

Visual Designer

Methods

data visualization
prototyping

Tools

Wally app / Excel / Tableau / Sketch / Principle

Story

In September 2014, I left my hometown to attend university that is 5 hours away. I have to pay all the living expenses using the money that my parents supported me, plus the money I earned from part time jobs.

To ensure I have enough money to live, I started to use spending tracing app to keep track on all of my expenses and income. I kept recording my spending it everyday, and eventually after 4 years I got all these spending data.

PROCESS
Collect - Visulize - Design

From September 2014 to January 2018, I recorded all my spending data in the Wally app, then I used Tableau to visualiza all the data and created the charts. Based on the charts, I created a data visualization dashboard design.

1 - COLLECT
Wally App

I used Wally App to record every single spending everyday, and use its exported function to get an excel spreadsheet.

wally app
exported data, with 1998 rows of data, 9 columns
2 - VISULIZE
Tableau Results

I put the excel data into Tableau, and selected 6 features that I want to visualize: category, month, food&drinks, transportation, housing, and aspect.

3 - DESIGN
Interactive Report
Reflection

In 2014, I was just a freshman of information management and I didn’t know that I’ll have the knowledge about data, information technology, and create designs now. I really enjoy the process looking back to all these thing I had and created, and integrated them into a new project.
